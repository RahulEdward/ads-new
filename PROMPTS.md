## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Be Specific**: More detail = better results
2. **Include Style References**: Mention brands or aesthetics you like
3. **Specify Colors**: Use hex codes or descriptive names
4. **Define Mood**: Energetic, calm, professional, playful
5. **Consider Platform**: Each platform has different optimal sizes
6. **Add Context**: Who is the audience? What's the goal?
